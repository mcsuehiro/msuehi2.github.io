Rent App UI

Designing a screening platform for the modern landlord

ABOUT

Rent Application is a tool for landlords to create and send digital rental applications and order screening reports potential tenants. In my redesign of the site, I created a new visual framework for the company.

Client

Rent Application

Role

Lead Designer

Contribution

Branding, Visual Design

Overview

I was hired by Rent Application to redesign their platform, from improving site architecture and user experience to creating a new brand identity and design system across the platform. To see the redesigned user experience, click here. As the lead designer I worked directly with the development team to bring the redesign to production.

Style Guide

I worked with the CEO to develop a tone for that fit with the brand. Through multiple iterations I created a comprehensive visual framework used throughout the entire platform.

Redesigning Screening Reports

In redesigning the framework, I redesigned all the tenant screening reports. In addition to giving the reports a visual facelift, I interviewed users to identify any areas of improvement in the redesign.

Visualizing Data

From my user interviews I discovered that landlord will need to decide on an applicant fast, so they need to evaluate an applicant in a simple scan of the document; I kept the credit score as the key focal element in the report; and using graphs to quickly encompass the applicant’s financial summary in a quick scan.

Redesigning the Homepage

Rent Application’s original homepage was visually cluttered and it’s value proposition was buried in too much text, their pricing section was confusing, and users didn’t have the patience to read through the whole page.
