Handshake Admin Redesign

Redesigning the backend admin system to streamline B2B merchant’s backend systems

ABOUT

Redesigning the backend admin system to streamline B2B merchant’s backend systems

Company

Handshake

Role

Lead Designer

Contribution

UX Strategy, Design, Research

The Problem

Handshake is a B2B ecommerce platform, and the Hub is the admin tool that allows merchants to manage their B2B business (Orders, customers, products)

The Hub is an admin tool that was built incrementally as new products and features were released from our product roadmap. Because of this, there was never time allocated to look at the product holistically, and this resulted in the backend system to be inefficient and confusing without hand holding from our customer success team

Goal

We wanted to improve the user experience of the admin site,********

  • Navigation
    Redesigning the navigation to make it more clear for users

  • Design system
    Establishing a scalable foundation

  • “Quick fixes”
    Making smaller improvements across the admin

Redesigning the Navigation

Challenges

The "Hub Navigation Redesign" was the most important update to be made on Hub. Our current navigation consisted only of 5 top level categories- one being the "set-up tab" often referred to as the “junk drawer”. Implementing a left hand navigation was driven first and foremost by the need to give our customers a clearer view of where they were within Hub at a given time.********* (REPLACE)
